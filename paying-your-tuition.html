Paying your Tuition

Have you recieved your Tuition Bill from Student Accounts and your wondering how to pay your remaining balance? There are a couple ways for you do to this!

  1. Take out a loan: You can either take out a Parent-PLUS loan, or go with a Private Loan- whichever works best with you and your family.
  2. Do a payment plan at the college: We have a few different payment plan options on a 3 month, 6 month or 9 month payment plan. You can work these details out with the Student Accounts Office
  3. You can do both! Want to take out a loan to pay for part of your tuition, but do a payment plan for the remaining balance? You certainly can do this!

If you have any questions about your financial aid package, or would like to discuss your payment options more you may contact our financial aid office at financialaid@aiken.edu or Student Accounts at studentaccounts@aiken.edu to schedule an appointment and someone will be in touch with you shortly!
