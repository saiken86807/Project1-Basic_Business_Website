Health Portal

What will I need to submit for documents in my health portal?

  • Proof of Insurance: Have a copy of your Insurance card to upload, be prepared to fill out a form with your membership number
  • Health History Form
  • Physical Exam: Form must be signed by your healthcare provider, and must have been within the last year as of September 2020
  • Copy of your Immunization Records

Please Note: If you have health insurance coverage you MUST waive the college issued health insurance plan each year, otherwise you will automatically be charged for it.

You can access the Health Portal here to upload all of your documents.
