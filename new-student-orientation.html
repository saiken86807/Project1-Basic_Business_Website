What will I need to Bring with me for my overnight stay for New Student Orientation?

  • Change of clothing, including sleepwear
  • Sneakers
  • Sweater or light jacket
  • Personal toiletries: Toothbrush, toothpast, shampoo, soap, towel
  • Prescription medication, if applicable

What should I NOT bring to New Student Orientation?

  • Expensive Jewlery
  • Laptop computers
  • Alcohol or other drugs- Please Note: Alcohol/drugs are prohibited and any negative behaviors and/or improper conduct could affect your standing with Aiken College.
